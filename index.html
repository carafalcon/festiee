What's Festiee?

Festiee is a new social game that allows users to earn points and redeem them towards various music festivals across the world. With the high innflation in ticket prices along with service charges, we feel that many people do not attend these major festivals due to the fact that tickets cost WAY to much. 

We want everyone to experience festival magic which is why we created this game to do just that.

Earn points, redeem tickets, enjoy.
